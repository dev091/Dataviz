# Launch Wedge Recommendation

## Recommended Wedge
Autonomous Executive Reporting and KPI Monitoring for:
- RevOps
- Finance
- Operations
- leadership teams

## Why This Wedge
This wedge has strong economics because it combines:
- high urgency
- recurring usage
- visible executive pain
- measurable labor replacement
- low need for long-tail visual parity

## Workflows To Solve First
- weekly executive reporting
- monthly business review packs
- KPI scorecards
- anomaly and threshold alerts
- board-style summary drafts
- recurring reporting workflows that currently depend on analysts

## What The Launch Product Must Do Well
- connect the core operating systems quickly
- clean and normalize messy data automatically
- infer initial business structure fast
- produce a trusted semantic KPI layer
- generate executive dashboards and report packs
- surface what changed and why
- route alerts and digests reliably

## What To Avoid In Launch
- long-tail chart parity
- open-ended BI customization
- collaboration sprawl
- marketplace strategy
- broad SMB self-serve motion
- high-support custom implementation

## Success Criteria For The Wedge
- first executive pack created in days, not months
- leadership reporting prep time reduced materially
- KPI monitoring becomes proactive instead of manual
- analytics teams maintain fewer recurring dashboards manually
- the first department expansion is obvious within the first 90 days

## Wedge Expansion Path
Once the wedge is proven, expansion should move outward through:
- more departments
- more governed metrics
- more automation routes
- stronger trust controls
- more migration tooling
